# Diana Design System v1

## Identity

Diana is a luxury editorial wedding design family inspired by timeless elegance, Italian villas, mikado fabrics, crystal glassware, champagne towers and refined details.

The bride values quality over trends.

The experience should feel calm, elegant, timeless and sophisticated.

---

## Core Principle

The wedding atmosphere is more important than the couple photo.

The website should first communicate mood, elegance and anticipation.

The couple photograph appears only after the Story section.

---

## Hero

No hero image.

Use an ivory background with a subtle paper or fabric texture.

Inspired by the original Diana wedding invitation.

Thin corner lines.

Large elegant serif names.

Minimalist layout.

Generous whitespace.

---

## Colors

Primary:
- Ivory
- Warm White

Secondary:
- Champagne
- Sand Beige

Accent:
- Antique Gold

Text:
- Charcoal

Avoid strong color changes between sections.

The entire website should feel like one consistent world.

---

## Photography

Photography is part of the design system.

Use large full-width editorial photographs between sections.

Preferred subjects:
- crystal glassware
- champagne tower
- Italian villas
- candlelight
- elegant table settings
- drapery
- mikado wedding dresses
- architectural details

Avoid excessive greenery or boho styling.

---

## Couple Photography

Do not show a couple photograph in the hero section.

The first couple photograph should appear only after the Story section.

---

## Decorative Elements

Use:
- thin corner lines
- subtle line-art flowers
- minimal floral accents

Decorative elements should support the layout, never dominate it.

---

## Floral Style

Flowers are secondary.

Preferred:
- delicate wildflowers
- line-art poppies
- simple botanical sketches

Avoid:
- watercolor floral frames
- large floral wreaths
- heavy botanical decoration
